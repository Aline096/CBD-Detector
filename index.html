Why Coffee Berry Diseases

Coffee plantations suffer from different diseases. Among the diseases, colletotrichum kahawae commonly known as coffee berry disease is the most occurring and vital one which affect coffee berry during its green stage and end up affecting coffee bean development and its quality.

Solution

Real-time system for early detection of coffee berry diseases

The aim is to assist farmers in recognizing coffee berry disease in the early stage which will help in timely detection of these diseases, resulting in increased coffee production output and quality.
